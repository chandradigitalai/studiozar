📄 StudioZar Service Agreement

By submitting this form, you agree to the following terms:

1. Scope of Work
The scope of work will be defined in the project proposal shared via WhatsApp/Email. Any additional features outside the agreed scope will be charged separately.

2. Payment Terms
50% advance payment is required to start the project. The remaining 50% is due upon completion and before final delivery. Payments are non-refundable once work has commenced.

3. Timeline
Estimated delivery timeline will be shared in the proposal. Delays caused by client feedback or third-party dependencies will extend the timeline accordingly.

4. Revisions
Up to 3 rounds of revisions are included in the quoted price. Additional revisions will be billed at ₹1,000 per round.

5. Ownership & IP
Full ownership of the final delivered product is transferred to the client upon full payment. StudioZar retains the right to display the work in its portfolio.

6. Confidentiality
All client information, data, and project details are kept strictly confidential and will not be shared with third parties.

7. Support
30 days of basic support (bug fixes) is included post-launch. Feature enhancements and ongoing maintenance are covered under separate maintenance plans.

8. Termination
Either party may terminate the agreement with 7 days written notice. In case of termination, payment for completed work is non-refundable.
